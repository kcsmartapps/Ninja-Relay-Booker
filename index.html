Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What exactly is KC Smart Auto Booker?

It’s a Chrome extension built to automate your work on the Amazon Relay load board. Instead of manually refreshing and booking for hours, the tool does it for you — helping you capture better-paying loads with far less effort.

Why should I use this tool?

Because it saves you time and maximizes your chances of success. We’re active Amazon Relay users ourselves, so we know the struggle of missing good loads. That’s why KC Smart Auto Booker is designed with real drivers and dispatchers in mind, and updated regularly to keep it reliable and effective.

How fast can I get started?

Immediately. Download the extension, follow the quick install steps, and you’ll be ready to go within minutes.

What’s the installation process like?

The setup is simple — once you download, you’ll see clear instructions.If you ever get stuck, our support team will happily sort things out for you.

Can I use the extension on more than one device?

NO. You can install it on a single computers or android device.

Does it work on phones or tablets?

Yes , our extensions works on both PC using (CHROME BROWSER) and Android devices using (KIWI BROWSER).

Is there any risk of getting banned on Amazon Relay?

We’ve added features like Safe Mode and smart randomization to minimize risk and keep your account safe. While Amazon may issue warnings if it notices unusual activity, our users rarely face issues — and so far the track record for bans is essentially zero.

Is billing monthly or yearly?

All subscriptions are billed monthly — no annual contract required.

How does renewal work?

Subscriptions renew automatically each month, so there’s nothing extra you need to do. If you’d like to manage your payments, you can view or adjust everything directly from your account dashboard.

Can I use one subscription across multiple companies?

No. Each subscription is tied to a single company. If you’re running more than one business, you’ll need a separate subscription for each.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Log into your account,Go to the Manage Subscription,Select the subscription you wish to end,Click Cancel.

Do you offer refunds?

No — all sales are final, and payments are non‑refundable.
